# Readynest_Internship_Week_3
# 🏹 Kurukshetra Business Intelligence & Digital Opportunity Analysis

> **Readynest Data Analytics Internship – Week 3 Project**

A Business Intelligence project built using **Power BI** to analyze Google Maps business data of **Kurukshetra**, identify businesses with limited digital presence, and provide data-driven recommendations for digital transformation.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing local business data collected from Google Maps to understand business distribution, customer engagement, and digital adoption.

The objective is to identify businesses that have strong customer ratings but lack an online presence, helping uncover opportunities for digital growth.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze business distribution across sectors and categories.
- Evaluate the digital presence of local businesses.
- Identify businesses without websites.
- Compare ratings and customer reviews.
- Discover digital transformation opportunities.
- Generate business recommendations using data insights.

---

# 💡 Key Insights

- A significant number of businesses still lack an official website.
- Several highly rated businesses have limited digital presence.
- Shopping & Retail contributes a major share of businesses.
- Customer engagement is strong across multiple sectors.
- Businesses with high ratings and no website represent ideal digital transformation opportunities.

---

# 🎯 Recommendations

- Develop websites for businesses without an online presence.
- Improve Google Business Profiles.
- Invest in Local SEO.
- Encourage customer reviews.
- Strengthen digital marketing strategies.
- Prioritize high-rated businesses for digital transformation.
